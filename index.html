About Us

WELCOME TO DIDAYA

FUMAR MACHINERY PTE LTD was established in 1987 to provide machinery, spares and repair services for the marine, shipping and ship-repairing industry. Within a short time, its business volume increased so tremendously that two subsidiary companies, FUMAR ENGINEERING PTE LTD and FUMAR MARINE SERVICES PTE LTD were incorporated.
